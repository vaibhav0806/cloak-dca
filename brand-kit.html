Usage Guidelines

Rules for using the cloak brand consistently.

Do
  • Always use lowercase "cloak" in the wordmark
  • Always include the accent-colored period
  • Use Manrope for all UI text
  • Use JetBrains Mono for numeric/data values
  • Maintain high contrast (dark backgrounds)
  • Use the accent color sparingly for emphasis
  • Keep the icon on a dark background
Don't
  • Capitalize "Cloak" or "CLOAK" in the logo
  • Remove or recolor the accent period
  • Place the logo on busy or colorful backgrounds
  • Stretch, rotate, or distort the logo
  • Use the accent color as a large background fill
  • Mix additional typefaces into the UI
  • Add drop shadows or effects to the wordmark
