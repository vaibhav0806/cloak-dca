## 1. Product Summary

### One-liner
**Private dollar-cost averaging on Solana.**

### Elevator Pitch
Cloak lets you DCA into crypto on Solana without anyone seeing your trades. Zero-knowledge proofs break the link between your wallet and your trading activity. Your strategy, timing, and positions stay completely private.

### The Problem
Every trade on Solana is public by default:
- Wallet balances are exposed to anyone
- DCA timing is visible to front-running bots
- Full transaction history is permanently trackable
- Copy traders can replicate your entire strategy

This means if you're accumulating SOL, BTC, or any token via DCA, everyone can see exactly what you're doing, when you're doing it, and how much.

### The Solution
Cloak routes your funds through a ZK-proof privacy pool before executing trades. Your real wallet never touches a DEX. The result:
- **Shielded balances** — nobody can see your holdings
- **Private execution** — trades happen from an unlinkable session wallet
- **Untraceable history** — deposits cannot be connected to trades

### Key Features
| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| Private | ZK proofs sever the link between your wallet and trades |
| Automated | Sign once, keeper bot executes all future trades |
| Self-custody | You keep your keys; Cloak facilitates trades |

### Supported Assets
- **Deposit:** USDC, USDT
- **Accumulate:** SOL, cbBTC (Coinbase wrapped BTC), ZEC (Zcash)
- **Frequencies:** Hourly, 4h, 12h, Daily, Weekly

### Current Status
**Private Beta** — invite code required. Early access via request.

### Links
- **Website:** usecloak.xyz
- **X/Twitter:** @cloakdefi
- **GitHub:** github.com/vaibhav0806/cloak-dca

## 2. Brand Identity & Voice

### Name
Always lowercase: **cloak.** (with the tomato-red period). Never "Cloak", never "CLOAK" in branding. In sentences, capitalize normally ("Cloak lets you...") but the logo/wordmark is always `cloak.`

### Tagline
> "The private way to accumulate crypto"

### Alternative taglines for rotation
- "Accumulate wealth quietly."
- "Your strategy. Your secret."
- "Invisible by design."
- "DCA without the audience."
- "Private by default."

### Tone & Voice

**Do:**
- Be confident and understated — quiet flex, not loud hype
- Use short, punchy sentences
- Be technical when it adds credibility (ZK proofs, not "magic privacy")
- Frame it as wealth-building, not trading ("accumulate" not "buy")
- Sound premium and intentional

**Don't:**
- Sound paranoid or fearful ("they're watching you!")
- Use hype language ("to the moon", "LFG", "wagmi")
- Over-explain — let the product speak
- Be preachy about privacy — state facts, let people decide
- Use emojis excessively (one max per tweet, if any)

### Key Vocabulary

| Use | Instead of |
|---|---|
| accumulate | buy, trade, swap |
| shielded | hidden, secret |
| private | anonymous (too loaded) |
| strategy | portfolio, bag |
| untraceable | anonymous |
| invisible | hidden |
| mathematically private | encrypted (more precise) |

### Visual Identity
- **Background:** pure black (#000000)
- **Text:** white (#ffffff)
- **Accent:** tomato red (#ff6347) — used sparingly for emphasis
- **Muted:** gray (#737373) — secondary info
- **Fonts:** Manrope (display/UI), JetBrains Mono (data/code)
- **Style:** dark, minimal, premium. Orbital rings, subtle glows, clean typography.

### Template B: "How It Works" Thread (Education)

```
1/ how does cloak make your DCA invisible?

a technical breakdown in plain english:

[thread]

2/ step 1: you deposit USDC into a shielded balance.

your deposit becomes a cryptographic commitment — a ZK proof that says "X amount was deposited" without revealing who deposited it.

3/ step 2: you configure your DCA strategy.

pick your token (SOL, BTC, ZEC), amount per trade, and frequency. then sign once with your wallet.

4/ step 3: the keeper bot takes over.

a server-side bot executes your trades automatically on schedule. it pulls from the privacy pool into a fresh session wallet.

5/ the key insight: your real wallet never interacts with a DEX.

the session wallet does all the swapping via jupiter. there's no on-chain link back to you.

6/ "but can't someone trace the privacy pool?"

no. that's where ZK proofs come in. the proof validates that the withdrawal is legitimate without revealing which deposit it came from.

7/ the result:

- your wallet: clean. no swap history.
- your strategy: invisible. no pattern to detect.
- your funds: safe. self-custody throughout.

8/ try it: usecloak.xyz (invite-only beta)
```
